Class 08: Electronic Output Devicess

3 minute read

I wanted to make two different things. One thing I did was make a little music player. I just didn't use a buzzer so I made a little recreation of the theme from Merry Go Round of Life from the film Howl's Moving Castle. It was a bit hard because I was figuring out the melody by ear by playing to song over and over and I didn't have a piano near me so I couldn't really check whether I was right until I uploaded the code.

For my final project I tried making a small demo of a robotic grabber controlled by a servo. I didn't have the materials to put it together so I held it together with my hand and hot glued the servo to the side of the box that would have been able to have the servo screwed in (we have micro servos, not regular ones).
