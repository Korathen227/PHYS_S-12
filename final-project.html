Final Project Proposals

3 minute read

Ideas

One idea I had is a piano book page turner, which would turn pages of my piano books while I play. Basically my left foot would press a small sensor at the bottom of the piano and then there would be a device at the top of the piano whih would turn the page.

One other idea I had was a drawing robot. Since I recently learned how to code NNs, I thought it would be cool to make art that is entirely computer generated -- basically the NN creates the artwork and then the drawing bot takes that image and draws it!

Another idea is to make a robotic hand that you will be able to control via a glove. Basically, this would entail having an actual hand hook up to a glove that would track the position of fingers and send that to the robotic hand so that it could mimic the same position. ROBOTIC ARM!!!

I eventually decided to build a robotic hand! Except, not really. It was a robotic arm. Although a hand would have been very cool and much more useful for surgery and other applications, it would be very difficult to make with the materials I have available. However, a robotic arm is much easier to make at a reasonable scale and it would be able to handle tools and such during surgery. Although I originally wanted to use soft robotics as a part of the robot it was not possible due to the fact that any one actuator takes ~16 hours to make, giving no room for trial and error given my short time frame. However, I want to pursue that afterwards. Anyways, this robot would also be great for other applications like annoying my brother remotely and also picking up very hot items.

Parts:

I am so grateful that my professors were able to send me laser cuts of a robotic CAD model I found from Thingiverse. I borrowed most of my materials (flex sensors, bluetooth modules, etc.) either from Amazon overnight shipping (it's free shipping! How is this free??) or from my friends who have lot of different gizmos and sensors. Notably, the things that weren't in the kit that I used were 5 regular-sized servos, 2 accelerometers, 3 adafruit flex sensors, 1 PCA9685 servo driver, and 2 HC-05 bluetooth modules which broke :(
